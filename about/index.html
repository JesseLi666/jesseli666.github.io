About Me 关于我

2019年3月16日

I’m now a first year graduate student in Renmin University of China School of Information. I’m also a member of DBIIR group, under the supervision of Prof. Jun He. I received my B.Sc. degree from Nanjing University, school of atmospheric sciences in 2018.

Currently my research interests include data mining and recommendation system, especially real-time recommendation. That’s to say, how to make the result of recommendation system change in real time when new user activities arrive.
